Key Features

  • Neural Activity Monitoring

    Constantly monitors the brain's neural activity to detect early signs of cyberpsychosis. Uses AI algorithms to predict potential onset based on user behavior and neural patterns.

  • Neurotransmitter Regulation

    Balances key neurotransmitters such as dopamine, serotonin, and norepinephrine to maintain mental stability. Provides targeted neural stimulation to correct imbalances.

  • Real-Time Feedback

    Offers real-time alerts and feedback to the user through a companion app or HUD (Heads-Up Display). Suggests behavioral adjustments and therapeutic exercises to mitigate symptoms.

  • Emergency Override

    In cases of acute cyberpsychosis, NeuroGuard can initiate an emergency override to stabilize the user's mental state. Connects to local medical facilities and cyberpsychosis management centers for immediate assistance.

  • Customizable Settings

    Allows users to customize their neural monitoring and treatment preferences. Adaptable to different types and levels of cybernetic augmentation.

  • Data Privacy and Security

    Ensures all neural data is encrypted and securely stored. Provides users with full control over their data and privacy settings.

Implementation in the Story

Discovery and Integration

During his investigation into the surge of cyberpsychosis cases in Navi Mumbai, Pramesh Chitrakar (ded_l0ck) stumbles upon the existence of NeuroGuard. He discovers that this advanced technology was created by a covert group of scientists and engineers who anticipated the potential dangers of excessive cybernetic augmentation.

How Pramesh Uses NeuroGuard:

  • Initial Research:

    Pramesh learns about NeuroGuard through hidden data files and leads from underground hacker networks.

  • Field Testing:

    He acquires a prototype of the device and tests it on himself and others experiencing early symptoms of cyberpsychosis.

  • Strategic Deployment:

    Pramesh uses NeuroGuard as a critical tool in his efforts to uncover the origins of the cyberpsychosis epidemic and to counteract the malicious forces manipulating the city's technological infrastructure.

  • Product Development:

    Throughout the story, Pramesh works to improve the NeuroGuard technology, making it more effective and accessible for those affected by cyberpsychosis.

Narrative Role:

  • Aid in Investigation: NeuroGuard helps Pramesh gather crucial data about the cyberpsychosis outbreaks.
  • Support for Allies: He uses the device to assist allies suffering from cyberpsychosis and protect the city’s inhabitants.
  • Tool for Confrontation: The emergency override feature of NeuroGuard becomes a key element in the climax of the story, allowing Pramesh to stabilize himself and others in high-stakes situations.
