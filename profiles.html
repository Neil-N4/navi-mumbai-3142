Pramesh Chitrakar (ded_l0ck)

Pramesh Chitrakar, also known by his alias 'ded_l0ck', is a skilled hacker and cybernetically enhanced vigilante in Navi Mumbai.
With his exceptional hacking abilities and cybernetic enhancements, Pramesh navigates the city's neon-lit streets and high-tech corridors to uncover the truth behind cyberpsychosis incidents plaguing the population. He discovers the NeuroShield technology and plays a pivotal role in its development and deployment.
